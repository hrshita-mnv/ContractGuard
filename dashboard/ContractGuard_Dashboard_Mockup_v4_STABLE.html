    About ContractGuard

    ContractGuard reduces manual contract reconciliation from hours to minutes, helping auditors and project managers catch costly billing discrepancies before payment goes out.

    This build cross-checks BOQ line items against RA Bills for GST/HSN mismatches, double-billed cumulative quantities, and rate drift away from locked BOQ rates.
